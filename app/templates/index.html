About the Site

The National Park Info Kiosk is a webapp that allows users to search for various information regarding national parks, including nearby campgrounds, visitor centers, places, as well as relevant people.

Use the search bar at the top to search for parks, campgrounds, or visitor centers by keyword. Just make sure to select which category you're filtering by! Otherwise, you can use the state filter at the bottom to see what parks and visitor centers are available in each state.
